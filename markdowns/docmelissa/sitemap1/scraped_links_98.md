# Resilient Leadership- Maneuvering Uncertainty

By [Doc Melissa](https://docmelissa.com/author/admin/ "Posts by Doc Melissa")April 4, 2020 [No Comments](https://docmelissa.com/resilient-leadership/#respond)




Discover how to maneuver uncertainty through becoming a resilient leader.

Join Keith Norris, Co- Captain of Paleo(fx) as he talks with Dr. Melissa in the Positive Impact Lab.

Keith and his wife Michelle are no strangers to disruption having already gone through the economic crash to rebuild their lives after 2008, yet the news they got early March of 2020 threw them for unexpected blow.  Their annual conference, Paleo(fx) that draws more than 10,000 attendees from around the globe was being told to cancel by the city of Austin, TX due to COVID-19.  Moving into immediate action to pivot, Michelle and the entire team within hours had found a solution that may have just saved the conference to happen mid-July instead of April as planned.

Tune in to discover how Keith and Michelle lead with resilience and co-create with their team to fully serve their tribe. But it doesn’t stop there… Keith pulls back the curtain to share his life lessons and the power in awaking to live his authentic story that is changing his own life and the lives of millions around the world.

The full podcast episode is available for download on iTunes, Stitcher, Spotify and Anchor.
